About

Hello, and welcome to my site, where you will find a selection of my work, a short resume and upcoming projects. There is a contact form available whether you simply want to say hi or discuss future films. Below are a few words about what I do.

Editing

Since 2010, most of my work has taken place in an editing room. After a training in post-production, and a year of assisting projects for a Paris-based production company, I was given the opportunity to become a chief editor for digital advertising films, tv shows & documentaries. From there, I gained experience and specialized in short-length digital films, which became my main activity. In parallel, I have participated in a number of fictional & musical productions, which have always taken a central part in my editing work, each production feeding on one another. Being an editor for more than 10 years in those specific areas, has given me various opportunities to train, practice, learn from others, and eventually shaped my own way of telling stories. I am happy to discuss any audio-visual project, from personal to corporate, as an advisor or editor, any time you wish to.

Directing

In 2018, I directed a first music video. It features a classic song by Amália Rodrigues, interpreted by a young French-Portuguese singer. It was a year and a half long project, based on a desire to create my own images for the first time, and a special love for the Fado music genre. The film was shot in Almada (Lisboa, Portugal), was partly post-produced in Paris, and remained entirely self-produced. It was an intense and rewarding process, which created a strong appeal for more. I am currently writing a first short film, and in the initial phase of a documentary work, both set in Portugal.

Filming

Since 2018, carried by an ever-growing love for image and sound, I started to explore a different aspect of storytelling, diversifying my audio-visual activities by entering the production & pre-production worlds. Rich of countless editing sessions and a recent directing experience, I have intensely trained in camera technique, practicing both still pictures & high-definition video capture. There are some examples of those on the « upcoming » section of this site, which will be updated shortly.
