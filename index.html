Directing

In 2018, I directed a first music video. It features a classic Fado song by Amália Rodrigues, which is interpreted by a young French-Portuguese singer, Tânia Raquel Caetano. She performs alongisde Lisbon-based musicians Múcio Sá and Nuno Estevens. The film was shot in Red 4K in Almada (Lisboa, Portugal).

The project originated from both a passion for the Fado music genre, its local Lisbon scene, its deep roots in Portuguese culture, and a strong desire to initiate a work in direction - which eventually became a first experience of production too. It was challenging but ultimately very rewarding to manage new aspects of filmmaking, going beyond its post-production aspect, and generated a strong appeal for more.
